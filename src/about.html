About A Helping of Greens

Empowering communities with fresh, nutrient-rich microgreens and sustainable food support.

Who We Are

A Helping of Greens is a community-focused nonprofit dedicated to providing microgreens, sustainable food support, and nutrition awareness to families and individuals. Our goal is simple: to promote healthier living through accessible food solutions.

What We Do

We collaborate with local growers, donors, volunteers, and community organizers to distribute fresh microgreens, run educational workshops, and support nutrition-focused initiatives.

  • • Food distribution programs
  • • Microgreen cultivation support
  • • Community nutrition education
  • • Fundraising campaigns for families in need

Our Core Values

Community

We prioritize people and connection. Every project is built around supporting our local communities.

Sustainability

We promote long-term, eco-friendly solutions that improve access to nutritious foods.

Impact

Every action is intentional — we aim to change lives through simple, scalable support systems.
